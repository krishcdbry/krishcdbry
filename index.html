SaveChey

Founder | Universal Bookmark Management

Cross-platform bookmark manager before cloud sync was mainstream. Bookmark anything and access anywhere – Android, iOS, iPad, web. Real-time sync across all devices with browser extensions and native apps.

The Problem: Bookmarks were locked to specific browsers and devices. Chrome bookmarks stayed in Chrome, Firefox in Firefox, mobile bookmarks didn't sync with desktop. People had fragmented bookmark collections scattered across devices with no unified way to access them.

The Vision: Built SaveChey to make bookmarks truly universal – independent of browsers, operating systems, or devices. One bookmark collection accessible everywhere, always in sync.

Platforms Built:

  • Web Platform – Full-featured web app with bookmark organization, tags, folders, and search
  • Android App – Native Android app with offline support and share integration
  • iOS App – Native iOS app with iCloud integration option
